Ironman 70.3 Asia - Pacific competition 2019

Share   

 

Till 2019, Ironman 70.3 Vietnam is 5 years old. In the past 4 years, the triathlon movement in Vietnam has been growing wildly in both quantity and quality. Ironman 70.3 Asia Pacific event in 2019 will be held in Danang City Vietnam. Specifically, the Danang Ironman 70.3 event in 2019 will take place on Sunday, May 12, 2019

For professional athletes from the Techcombank Ironman 70.3 Da Nang 2019, Vietnam – a prize for professional athletes worth 75,000 USD. Besides, the tournament will have 50 entries for the Ironman World Tournament 70.3 Da Nang 2019 in Nice City (France). With a larger scale, the number of bonus points for athletes with high rankings will also be greater. Therefore, the prize promises to attract many of the world’s top triathlete to Da Nang to compete in this event.
