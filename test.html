Vietnam

General information:

Vietnam is a Southeast Asian country known for its beaches, rivers, Buddhist pagodas and bustling cities. Hanoi, the capital, pays homage to the nation’s iconic Communist-era leader, Ho Chi Minh, via a huge marble mausoleum. Ho Chi Minh City (formerly Saigon) has French colonial landmarks, plus Vietnamese War history museums and the Củ Chi tunnels, used by Viet Cong soldiers....

Capital: Hanoi
Dialing code:+84
Population: 96.46 million (2019)


Best places to see in Vietnam
North of Vietnam: Hanoi - Halong Bay - Ninh Binh - Ba Be lake - Mai Chau - Pu Luong - Sa Pa - Bac Ha - Cat Ba island - Lan Ha bay - Ha Giang - Nghia Lo
Center of Vietnam: Hue - Hoi An - Da Nang - Nha Trang - Da Lat - Phong Nha
South of Vietnam: Hochiminh City - Phu Quoc Island - Ben Tre - Cai Be - Chau Doc - Cat Tien Park - Can Tho
